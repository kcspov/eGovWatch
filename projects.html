National Cybersecurity Plan 2022

72%
Implementing Agency: DICT

A comprehensive national strategy to secure the Philippines' cyberspace by strengthening the cybersecurity posture of government, businesses, and citizens. The plan focuses on building resilient digital infrastructure, developing cybersecurity workforce, and establishing a robust legal and regulatory framework.

Launch Date

May 2, 2017

Target Completion

December 2028

Key Goals:
  • Establish National CERT operations
  • Develop cybersecurity standards for government systems
  • Train 10,000+ cybersecurity professionals
72%

Free Wi-Fi for All Program

65%
Implementing Agency: DICT

A flagship government program that provides free internet access in public places including parks, plazas, public libraries, government hospitals, health centers, train stations, airports, and seaports across the Philippines. Aims to bridge the digital divide and promote digital inclusion for all Filipinos.

Launch Date

August 10, 2017

Target Completion

December 2028

Key Goals:
  • Install 25,000+ Wi-Fi hotspots nationwide
  • Cover all provinces and major municipalities
  • Provide minimum 10 Mbps connection speed
65%

National Broadband Plan

58%
Implementing Agency: DICT

A national strategy to accelerate the deployment of fiber optic cables and broadband infrastructure throughout the country. The plan aims to connect all government offices, schools, hospitals, and underserved communities to high-speed internet, enabling digital services and economic growth.

Launch Date

July 18, 2017

Target Completion

December 2028

Key Goals:
  • Deploy a national fiber backbone network
  • Achieve broadband connectivity in all 81 provinces
  • Reduce internet costs by 50%
58%

PhilSys (National ID)

80%
Implementing Agency: PSA / DICT

The Philippine Identification System (PhilSys) aims to establish a single national identification system for all citizens and resident aliens. The PhilSys ID serves as a valid proof of identity for public and private transactions, simplifying processes and promoting financial inclusion for all Filipinos.

Launch Date

August 6, 2018

Target Completion

December 2027

Key Goals:
  • Register 92 million+ Filipino citizens
  • Deliver physical and digital ID cards nationwide
  • Integrate with government and financial services
80%

DigitalJobsPH

70%
Implementing Agency: DICT / DOLE

A collaborative government initiative to equip Filipinos with digital skills for freelancing and online employment. The program provides free training in web development, graphic design, virtual assistance, digital marketing, and other in-demand ICT skills, empowering Filipinos to compete in the global digital economy.

Launch Date

March 15, 2016

Target Completion

Ongoing Program

Key Goals:
  • Train 1 million+ Filipinos in ICT skills
  • Establish training centers in all regions
  • Connect graduates with global freelancing platforms
